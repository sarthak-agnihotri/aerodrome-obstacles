Methods for Analyzing Aerodrome Obstacles

Aerodrome obstacle analysis is crucial for ensuring the safety of aircraft during takeoff and landing. Below are key methods used to assess potential obstacles.

Area Analysis Method

This method involves evaluating a defined area around the aerodrome to identify potential obstacles such as buildings, terrain, and vegetation.

Learn More

Flight Track Analysis Method

Focuses on analyzing aircraft flight paths during takeoff and landing to assess potential interferences with terrain or man-made structures.

Learn More

Use of GIS Tools

Geographic Information Systems help visualize and analyze obstacles in relation to aircraft operations, enabling smarter planning and obstacle management.
