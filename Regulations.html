Regulations for Aerodrome Obstacles

Regulations play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of aircraft operations at aerodromes. They provide guidelines for managing obstacles that may affect flight paths.

Key Regulatory Bodies

International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO)

Sets global aviation standards.

Federal Aviation Administration (FAA)

Regulates aviation safety in the United States.

European Union Aviation Safety Agency (EASA)

Oversees aviation safety in Europe.

Specific Regulations

Obstacle Limitation Surfaces (OLS)

Defines maximum obstacle heights around aerodromes.

Approach and Departure Procedures

Regulations for safe flight paths during takeoff and landing.

Safety Zones

Guidelines for structures permitted near aerodromes.

Environmental Regulations

Considerations for noise and wildlife management.
